Not veterinary advice

PetPickHQ is not a substitute for veterinary care. Everything here is general information and lived experience to help you care for an aging dog. It is not a diagnosis, a treatment plan, or medical advice for your specific dog.

Please talk to your own vet

Always consult a licensed veterinarian about your dog’s health — especially before starting any supplement, medication, or new routine, and any time you notice a sudden change, pain, or distress. Never give your dog human medications unless your vet has prescribed them; many are dangerous or fatal to dogs.

No cure or treatment claims

I do not claim that any product or approach treats, cures, or prevents any condition. I describe comfort measures and share what helped (or didn’t help) my own dog. Results vary from dog to dog. In an emergency, contact your veterinarian or a 24-hour animal hospital immediately.
